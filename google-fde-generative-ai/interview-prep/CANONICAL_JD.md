## ROLE — what an FDE at Google Cloud actually does

**One-sentence definition (verbatim, appears across 8 of 10 postings):**

> "An embedded builder who bridges the gap between frontier AI products and production-grade reality within customers."

**Variant framings used interchangeably:**

- "**Builder-consultant**" (DACH, Singapore, GenMedia)
- "**Innovator-builder**" (Japan, GenMedia)
- "**High-agency engineer with a founder's mindset**" (India, GenMedia, Staff)
- "**Hybrid professional**, blending core engineering with customer engagement" (US generic, London, Telecom)
- "**Agent engineer and primary delivery arm**" (Applied AI variant)

**The dual purpose (verbatim on 7 of 10 postings):**

1. "**White-glove deployment** of (complex) AI systems" within the customer's environment
2. "**Critical feedback loop**, transforming real-world field insights into Google Cloud's future product roadmap"

**The production-blockers you solve (verbatim):**

- Integration complexities
- Data readiness issues
- State-management challenges
- *"…that prevent AI from reaching enterprise-grade maturity."*

**Delivery style (Applied AI / Telecom variants):**

- **"High-travel, high-impact"** — "significant customer collaboration and travel often required"
- "Lead with deep, **bespoke implementation** as the primary value proposition"
- "Translate one-off customer solutions into **reusable, scalable assets**"

## RESPONSIBILITIES — universal across postings

Four responsibility bullets appear on almost every posting, near-verbatim:

1. **Lead developer for AI applications** — "Transitioning from rapid prototypes to production-grade agentic workflows (e.g., **multi-agent systems, Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers**) that drive **measurable ROI**."
2. **Architect and code the connective tissue** — "Between Google's AI products and customer's **live infrastructure**, including **APIs, legacy data silos, and security perimeters**."
3. **Build evaluation pipelines and observability frameworks** — "To ensure agentic systems meet rigorous requirements for **accuracy, safety, and latency**."
4. **Convert field insights into product requests** — "Identify repeatable field patterns and technical friction points in Google's AI stack, converting them into **reusable modules or formal product feature requests** for the engineering teams."

**Variant-specific additional responsibilities:**

- **Applied AI variant:** "Take initial conversational prototypes and transform them into production-ready solutions, owning the engineering lifecycle, including the transition from 'art of the possible' to real-world business value." Also: establish first **Customer User Journeys (CUJs)** for largest customers at their sites.
- **Telecom variant:** "Construct rapid prototype Generative AI applications tailored to Google Cloud customers ranging from early stage startups to prominent established companies."
- **London variant:** Explicit mention of **"pipelines for structured, unstructured data, incorporating vector databases and RAG-like architectures."**
- **Staff variant:** Add architectural-governance responsibilities — **design the security/integration standards** that junior FDEs use.

## MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS — the hard gate

The minimum bar **varies by seniority tier**. Here's the honest matrix across all variants:

### Early (FDE I) — DACH, Japan, some US

- Bachelor's in STEM or equivalent practical experience
- **2–3 years** in Python, typical ML packages (keras, HF transformers)
- Some experience with building on pretrained models (prompt engineering, fine-tuning, RAG, tool orchestration)
- *Sometimes:* Experience implementing multi-agent systems using LangGraph/CrewAI and patterns like ReAct, self-reflection, hierarchical delegation

### Mid (FDE, Applied AI) — US generic

- Bachelor's in CS or equivalent
- **3+ years** in Python and architecting AI systems on cloud platforms
- **3+ years** developing full-stack applications integrated with **enterprise IT infrastructures (ERP, CRM, legacy DBs)** and managing technical projects
- **2+ years** developing conversational agents using frameworks (LangChain, Dialogflow CX, Rasa)
- Experience deploying resources via **Terraform** or similar IaC

### Senior (FDE Telecom, Singapore Mandarin) — your likely band

- Bachelor's + **6–8 years** production-grade AI
- Experience in **client-facing technical roles**
- Experience in applied AI with foundation models (prompt engineering, fine-tuning, RAG, tool orchestration)
- Experience **architecting, deploying, or managing solutions on a cloud platform**
- **Experience leading technical discovery sessions with executive stakeholders (C-suite)** and engineering teams to define AI and hardware infrastructure requirements
- *Telecom:* 8 years in Python or similar (Java, C++, Go)
- *Singapore:* Mandarin fluency (language variant)

### Staff (Staff FDE) — US advanced

- Master's or PhD preferred, but not strictly required
- 10+ years production AI; 5+ years customer-facing
- Prior experience architecting multi-customer reference implementations
